Stop Starvation

Currently, there is a global food crisis, and it is the largest one in modern history. Due to the cause of new conflicts, weather, and climate changes, the number of people starving are increasing everyday. In 2021 itself, 828 million people were suffering from starvation. This means that nearly 10% percent of the world population is starving. Every day, about 25,000 people perish bacause of starvation.

Sub Saharan Africa

In Sub Sharan Africa, Starvation is the worst it could get. The population is rapidly increasing which is also affecting the amount of starved people.
